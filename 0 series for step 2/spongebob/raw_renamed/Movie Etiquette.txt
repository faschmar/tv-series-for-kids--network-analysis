==Movie Version==
{{L|''[The Nickelodeon logo appears in front of Bikini Atoll. The camera pans below the water, where the Reef Cinema is showing a film called "A Sea Star Is Born."]''}}
{{L|French Narrator|Ah, ze cinema. A magical place for all to enjoy, so long as we follow a few simple rules.}}
{{L|Squidward|''[He hums and walks into the theater. He sees an open seat.]'' Oh! Ah.}}
{{L|SpongeBob|Oh, hi, Squidward!}}
{{L|Squidward|Grr...}}
{{L|French Narrator|In case of emergency, always take a moment to locate your nearest exit...}}
{{L|''[Patrick slams the exit door on an usher.]''}}
{{L|French Narrator|And try to find your seat before the film begins.}}
{{L|Patrick|Excuse me! ''[He sits near Squidward.]''}}
{{L|Squidward|Huh? ''[He grumbles angrily.]''}}
{{L|French Narrator|Please do not disrupt the film for others. This means no gum chewing.}}
{{L|''[Patrick loudly chews gum and blows a bubble in Squidward's face.]''}}
{{L|French Narrator|No bouncing.}}
{{L|''[SpongeBob bounces up and down on his seat, annoying Squidward.]''}}
{{L|French Narrator|No armrest hogging.}}
{{L|''[Patrick hogs the armrest, intruding on Squidward's space.]''}}
{{L|French Narrator|No jellyfishing.}}
{{L|''[A jellyfish swims by. SpongeBob catches it and Squidward in his jellyfishing net. It zaps Squidward, and he screams.]''}}
{{L|SpongeBob|Whoops! Sorry!}}
{{L|French Narrator|No hamboning.}}
{{L|Patrick|Ooh, ooh, ooh, ooh! ''[He slaps his knees and chest.]''}}
{{L|French Narrator|No gargling.}}
{{L|SpongeBob|''[He loudly gargles in front of Squidward.]''}}
{{L|French Narrator|No play fighting.}}
{{L|''[SpongeBob and Patrick play-fight with each other.]''}}
{{L|Squidward|Sit down and stop ruining my night off!}}
{{L|''[A larger, muscular usher grabs Squidward and clenches his head real tight.]''}}
{{L|French Narrator|And of course, no talking.}}
{{L|''[The usher takes Squidward out of the theater. SpongeBob and Patrick shrug.]''}}
{{L|SpongeBob|Enjoy the movie, Patrick!}}
{{L|Patrick|Shh! You're talking!}}
